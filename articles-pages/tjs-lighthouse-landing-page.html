Core Capabilities

What the Lighthouse Does

Every feature runs autonomously. TJ does his normal work. The system handles the intelligence, organization, and outreach.

🎙

Meeting Intelligence

Every Zoom call is recorded by Fathom. The bot auto-pulls transcripts, extracts decisions, action items, contacts, and revenue opportunities. Writes everything to CRM and Obsidian vault.

🧠

Feed the Brain

Drop any URL in Discord. Get back AI analysis in 60 seconds: key insights, which business it applies to, and a specific action plan. YouTube, articles, newsletters.

📧

Daily Briefing

6 AM email with synthesized priorities, revenue pipeline, content ideas, stale contacts needing follow-up, and agent activity. 7 AM task list organized by urgency.

🔍

AI Sales Agents

Lenny researches real businesses via Google Places API. Carl drafts 50-75 word personalized cold pitches using 7 different angles. Approve or reject with a Discord reaction.

📰

Newsletter Processing

Gmail newsletters automatically pulled overnight from 4 labels, run through AI analysis, and saved to the intelligence vault. Fresh insights ready for the morning brief.

📚

Knowledge Vault

Everything writes to three places: Supabase (queries), Obsidian (permanent knowledge, synced to all devices via GitHub), and Discord (real-time visibility). Nothing is lost.
