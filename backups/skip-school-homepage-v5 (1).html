Prompt of the Week

Turn Any Obsession Into a Full Week of Learning

My 7-year-old spent three days talking about volcanoes. Instead of fighting it, I turned it into our whole week.
You are a creative homeschool curriculum
designer. My child is [AGE] years old
and is obsessed with [INTEREST].

Create a one-week unit study connecting
their interest to:
- Math (real-world problems)
- Science (experiments or concepts)
- Reading (book recs at their level)
- Writing (one fun project)
- History or Geography connection

Make it feel like play, not school.
Include one field trip idea and one
hands-on project.

Keep each day's plan to 3-4 hours of
active learning. Be specific with book
titles, experiment supplies, and math
problem examples.
