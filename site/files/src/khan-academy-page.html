What It's Best For

Math, without question. The progression from counting through calculus is thorough. Exercises are well-designed, and mastery-based means your child doesn't advance until they actually understand the concept.

Independent practice time. When you're teaching one child and need the other to work independently, Khan Academy provides structured, adaptive practice without your supervision.

How We Use It

Our daughter does 20-30 minutes of Khan Academy math four days a week. Khanmigo handles the "I'm stuck" moments when I'm busy with her brother. On Fridays, I check the parent dashboard to plan the next week. Total cost: $44/year for Khanmigo. The free tier works fine, but $3.67/month for the AI tutor is worth it.
